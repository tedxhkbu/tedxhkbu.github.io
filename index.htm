David & Goliath
The Surprising Strength

Event Details

Date

March 29th, 2014

Time

12:30 pm ~ 6:30 pm

Location

Dr Hari Harilela Lecture Theatre (WLB 103), HKBU

Speakers

featuring the best fighters

"David and Goliath" is the theme of this year's TEDxHKBU, illustrating the idea of the "Surprising Strength". Speakers all come from a wide diversity of cultures and backgrounds, yet they share one thing in common - encounter with the giants.

Chia-liang Kao
Co-Founder of g0v.tw

Mr. Chia-liang Kao is a computer programmer who loves hotspring. He is a co-founder of g0v.tw, an online community that advocates information transparency and focuses on developing information platform and tools for the citizens to participate in society. g0v.tw is based on the open-source spirit to promote freedom of speech and information openness. It enables citizens to understand government operations and subjects under discussion more easily and clearly. Thus, information would not be solely controlled by the media. Citizens' supervision of the government can be made more effective.

高嘉良
零時政府聯合創辦人

高嘉良先生是一名自由軟件程式員，喜愛溫泉。2012年與友人發起零時政府項 目。零時政府是一個推動資訊透明的社群，致力於開發公民參與社會的資訊平 台與工具。零時政府以開放原始碼的精神為基底，關心言論自由及資訊開放， 透過編寫程式設計一個容易使用的資訊服務。資訊的透明化能幫助公民更確實 地了解政府的運作，更快速地了解相關的政治議題。公開透明的資訊，不受媒 體的壟斷，因而讓大衆能夠更有效地監督政府。

Christina Dean
Founder & CEO of Redress

Dr. Christina Dean is the Founder and CEO of Redress, an NGO with a mission to promote environmental sustainability in the fashion industry. Christina is a regular speaker at seminars and has received numerous recognitions for her work, including being listed by U.S. online magazine Coco Eco as one of ‘2010’s Most Influential Women in Green’ and by U.K. Vogue as one of the U.K.’s ‘Top 30 Inspirational Women’. Prior to founding Redress, Christina was a journalist and a practicing dental surgeon.

丁潔絲
Redress創辦人及行政總裁

丁潔絲博士是Redress的創辦人及行政總裁。Redress是一個推動時裝業可持續 發展的非牟利團體。丁博士曾多次獲邀出席不同講座分享自己的理念，其對 可持續性發展時裝的貢獻更屢獲各界認同，包括被美國網上雜誌Coco Eco選為 「2010年最具影響力的環保女士」之一，被英國雜誌Vogue選為「英國首30位最 具啟發性的女性」之一。在成立Redress前，丁博士曾是一位記者及執業牙醫。

David Yeung
Co-Founder of Green Monday

Mr. David Yeung is the co-founder of Green Monday, a social enterprise that promotes healthy and environmental-friendly living in Hong Kong. Mr. Yeung is the author of best-selling books《Go Green餐廳88間》, 《開工正能量》, and《棟篤禪》. He is a regular columnist on East Week, Metro Newspaper, and Buddhist Compassion, as well as a radio program host on Metro Radio. Mr. Yeung was selected as ‘Men of 2013’ by JMEN, ‘Local Heroes 2013’ by Ming Pao Weekly, Bronze Award Winner of ‘Award for Marketing Excellence’ by Hong Kong Management Association, and ‘Best Idea of 2013’ by U magazine.

楊大偉
Green Monday聯合創辦人

楊大偉先生是社會企業Green Monday聯合創辦人，致力宣揚健康、環保、低 碳的生活方式。楊先生亦是暢銷書《Go Green餐廳88間》、《開工正能量》和 《棟篤禪》作者，《東周刊》、《溫暖人間》、《都市日報》等專欄作者， 香港新城財經台節目主持，獲JMEN頒發「Men of 2013」大獎、《明報周刊》 「2013 Local Heroes」大獎、香港管理專業協會「2013傑出市場策畫」銅獎、 《東周刊》「2013傑出企業策畫」大獎、U Magazine「Best Idea of 2013」大獎 等。

Prudence Mak
Founder & Chief Designer of Chocolate Rain

Ms. Prudence Mak graduated in Fine Art and was granted the Design Smart scholarship to Central Saint Martin’s London for M.A. Degree in Design Studies. She received the 2010 Ten Outstanding Designers Award, was honoured as one of Hong Kong’s Ten Outstanding Young Persons, amongst numerous other international awards. Prudence is the founder and chief designer of Chocolate Rain, a design inspired by childhood dreams and wonderland adventures. Each piece is unique and handcrafted with mixed medium and special artistic elements. Apart from being showcased in flagship stores in various countries, her artworks have been exhibited internationally, including the MoMA, Tate Modern and Guggenheim Museum. Furthermore, Chocolate Rain design’s collections and illustrations were commissioned projects for some global brands to spread messages of conservation and care.

麥雅端
Chocolate Rain始創人及設計總監

麥雅端小姐畢業於加拿大亞伯塔大學藝術系及倫敦中央聖馬丁設計學院，為本 地創作人。麥小姐除了是香港十大傑出設計師和香港十大傑出青年外，更獲多 個國際獎項。她於2002年創辦Chocolate Rain，設計靈感源自孩童的夢想及 奇妙的旅程，獨一無二的設計和親手製作的手工是其最大的特色。Chocolate Rain曾在世界各地展出，如古根漢博物館、大英博物館等，亦在多個國家擁有 旗艦店，並常與國際品牌、慈善機構和環保組織等機構合作，以不同媒界宣揚 保育、關愛的訊息。

Pearl Tse
Founder of Togetherness - Life & Death Education

Ms. Pearl Tse is an educator and the founder of Togetherness, an NGO providing life and death education. She is also one of the Executive Committee members of Expressive Arts Therapy Association of Hong Kong. Via expressive art as well as life and death workshops, Pearl works toward the goal of educating the public to pursue a fuller and more contented life by a comprehensive and in-depth self-reflection. She has provided training to chronically ill patients, home alone elderly, ex-convicts, mental illness patients, teenage mothers, bereaved persons, rehabilitation counselors, students, and professionals with different backgrounds, etc.

謝敏如
同行力量創辦人

謝敏如小姐，教育工作者，生死教育組織同行力量的創辦人。她同時創立了一 個表達藝術的空間，名為人本澡堂，也是香港表達藝術治療協會執行委員會的 成員。謝小姐透過表達藝術以及生死探索工作坊，讓大眾對生命有更全面和深 入的反思，學習更積極的去面對逆境以達致整全滿足的人生。謝小姐的服務對 象廣泛，包括長期病患者、獨居長者、更生人士、精神病患者、未成年母親、 喪親人士、復康機構社工、學生及不同背景的在職人士等。

Wilson Cheung
Polar Expedition Guide

Mr. Wilson Cheung graduated from Hong Kong Baptist University in 2010 and continued his Master’s degree in Transcultural European Outdoor Studies in Europe. Mr. Cheung is the co-founder of Ecobus and the only Chinese polar expedition tour guide in the world. He got the recommendation from Dr. Rebecca Lee and was successfully selected as the sponsorship recipient among 2,000 applicants from the U.S. environmental organization 2041 to go on an Antarctic expedition. Mr. Cheung endeavours to arouse the awareness of public toward global warming by sharing his experiences.

張偉賢
極地探險導賞員

張偉賢先生於2010年畢業於香港浸會大學，及後於歐洲攻讀跨文化戶外教育研 究碩士課程，亦為環保組織生態巴士Ecobus創辦人之一，更是現時全球唯一一 位華裔極地探險導賞員。張先生從小喜愛到不同國家遊歷，在大學時，他更獲 李樂詩博士推薦，從2000 多人中脫穎而出，成功獲得美國環保組織 2041的贊 助，前往南極探險。張先生希望能以生命影響生命，將全球暖化信息推廣到社 區，喚起公眾關注。

Zhou Chen
Motivational Speaker

Mr. Zhou Chen is a motivational speaker and singer. He had a difficult childhood and lost his legs due to an unfortunate accident at the age of thirteen. After a tough time making ends meet through polishing shoes and selling newspapers, he eventually became a singer. He has been to more than 700 cities in China and has had over 3,000 performances. Mr. Chen has also been actively participating in charity work via non-paid performance for minority groups, donating over 500 thousand dollars, and hired by many organizations and schools as a motivational trainer. Mr. Chen has a positive attitude towards life and passion in mountaineering. In 2012, he climbed up the Five Sacred Mountains in China (cumulative elevation was 8498 meters).

陳州
勵志演說家

陳州先生是一位勵志演說家及歌手。6歲父母離異，8歲流浪四方，13歲從火車 上摔下失去雙腿。後來經歷過乞討、賣報紙、擦皮鞋，最終成為一名流浪歌 手，用歌聲走遍中國700多個城市，演出超過3000場。陳先生同時熱心公益，為 弱勢群體義務演出、演講，捐獻善款超過50多萬元，更被多個機構和學校聘請 為勵志導師。陳先生熱愛生活，熱愛登山。2012年，他依靠雙手登頂中國五嶽 （海拔總和8498米）。

Silvain Gilbert
Jewish Holocaust Survivor

Mr. Silvain Gilbert is a Jewish born in Belgium. He has been living in Hong Kong for 18 years. During the Jewish Holocaust, Silvain was adopted as a child by Tante Fanny, a courageous Catholic lady. As a Holocaust survivor, Silvain regularly tours schools in Hong Kong sharing his experiences with students. His primary source of testimony is a powerful tool in Holocaust education. He enables the public to have a better understanding of the WWII holocaust history. In 2014, his autobiography a lost word, A NEW LIFE will be published.

季思宏
猶太大屠殺倖存者

季思宏先生是一名出生於比利時的猶太人，已在香港居住了18年。二戰時被勇 敢的天主教徒Tante Fanny女士收養，得以逃離納粹德軍對猶太人的大屠殺。他 經常以大屠殺倖存者的身份，到本港學校分享經歷，令不少人大受感動，同時 對二戰大屠殺時的歷史有更深的認識。他的自傳a lost word, A NEW LIFE 將於 2014年出版。

Siu Hung Ng
Advertising Columnist / Partner of Metta Communications

Mr. Siu Hung Ng is the author of best-selling advertising books, the Ad101 series. He is not only a regular columnist for newspapers and websites, but also a passionate guest speaker for universities to share advertising experience with students. Mr. Ng is the co-founder and partner of Metta Communications, one of the most active local advertising agencies in Hong Kong in recent years. He has devoted himself to the industry and worked on the strategies for a number of global and local brands. His current popular advertising campaigns include Mannings, Fujifilm, and BUPA, etc.

胡若藍
廣告寫作人 / Metta合夥人

季胡若藍先生活躍於廣告寫作，曾參與出版多本香港最暢銷的廣告書籍，如《廣 告101》系列。現時每周於本地報章及知名網站撰寫廣告專欄，並經常出席香港 各大學的廣告論壇及研討會，在會中與大學生分享廣告創作經驗。他創立了近 年最活躍的本地廣告公司Metta Communications並作為其合夥人之一。胡先生 於4As廣告公司工作多年，曾參與逾百個國際及本地品牌的廣告工作。目前他參 與的流行廣告項目包括萬寧、富士相機和保柏醫療保險等。

About TED/TEDx

Ideas worth spreading

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience.

At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event.

The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. Started as a four-day conference in California 25 years ago, TED has grown to support those world-changing ideas with multiple initiatives.

The annual TED Conference invites the world’s leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes. Their talks are then made available, free, at TED.com.

TED speakers have included Bill Gates, Al Gore, Jane Goodall, Elizabeth Gilbert, Sir Richard Branson, Nandan Nilekani, Philippe Starck, Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala, Isabel Allende and former UK Prime Minister Gordon Brown.

The annual TED Conference takes place each spring in Long Beach, California, along with the TEDActive simulcast in Palm Springs; the annual TEDGlobal conference is held each summer in Edinburgh, Scotland.

TED's media initiatives include TED.com, where new TEDTalks are posted daily, the recently launched TED-Ed platform for students and educators.
