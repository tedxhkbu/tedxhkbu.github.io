Speakers

featuring the best fighters

"David and Goliath" is the theme of this year's TEDxHKBU, illustrating the idea of the "Surprising Strength". Speakers all come from a wide diversity of cultures and backgrounds, yet they share one thing in common - encounter with the giants.

Chia-liang Kao
Co-Founder of g0v.tw

Mr. CL KAO is a computer programmer who loves cats. He is a co-founder of g0v.tw, an online community that advocates information transparency and focuses on developing information platform and tools for the citizens to participate in the society. g0v.tw is based on open-source spirit to promote freedom of speech and information openness. It enables citizens to understand its government operation and subjects under discussion more easily and clearly.

Christina Dean
Founder & CEO of Redress

Dr. Christina Dean is the founder and CEO of Redress, who is also an active speaker promoting social responsibility in fashion industry. Christina has received numerous recognitions for her work, including being listed by US online magazine Coco Eco as one of ‘2010’s Most Influential Women in Green’ and by UK Vogue as one of the UK’s ‘Top 30 Inspirational Women’. Before founding Redress, Christina was a journalist and a practicing dental surgeon.

David Yeung
Co-Founder of Green Monday

Mr. David YEUNG is the co-founder of Green Monday, a non-profit social enterprise that is committed to encourage healthy and environmental-friendly living in Hong Kong. It starts with promoting meat-free diet on Monday and encourages corporations to go green within the business scope. Mr. Yeung is a columnist on East Week, Metro Newspaper, Buddhist Compassion, and the author of his book 《棟篤禪》and《開工正能量》. He is also host of a program on Metro Radio, has given talks at numerous organizations and academic institutions.

Prudence Mak
Founder & Chief Designer of Chocolate Rain

Ms. Purdence MAK is graduated in Fine Art and granted the Design Smart scholarship to Central Saint Martin’s London for MA Degree in Design Studies. She has received the 2010 Ten Outstanding Designers Award, honored as one of Hong Kong’s Ten Outstanding Young Persons as well as won numerous international awards. Ms. Purdence is the founder and designer of Chocolate Rain. Chocolate Rain Design is inspired by childhood dream and wonderland adventure. Each piece is unique and handcrafted with mix medium and special artistic elements.

Pearl Tse
Founder of Togetherness - Life & Death Education

Ms. Pearl TSE was graduated from University of Newcastle-upon-Tyne in Education and holds a mast degree from The University of Hong Kong in Behavioral Health. Ms. Pearl is the founder and trainer of “Togetherness”, a NGO providing life and death education to general public for a more comprehensive and in-depth reflection on life, in order to lead to a fuller and more contented life as well as showing more respect and appropriate care for others. She is the pioneer in promoting life & death education in Hong Kong.

Wilson Cheung
Polar Expedition Guide

Mr. Wilson CHEUNG was graduated from Hong Kong Baptist University’s in 2010 and continued his Master’s degree in Transcultural European Outdoor Studies in Europe. Mr. Cheung is the only Chinese Arctic expedition tour guide in the world and also the co-founder of Ecobus. He got the recommendation from Dr. Rebecca Lee and successfully selected as the sponsorship recipient among 2,000 applicants from the US environmental organization ‘2041’ to go on an Antarctic expedition in his university days. Mr. Cheung hopes arousing the awareness of public toward global warming by sharing his experiences.

Zhou Chen
Motivational Speaker

Mr. Zhou CHEN, originated from Shandong Linyi. Unfortunately he lost his legs due to an accident when he was thirteen. He had a tough time on his daily living through polishing shoes, selling newspapers, fixing electronic appliance, and, eventually, he became a singer. He once said "I can walk when I sing." has touched the heart of countless audience. Mr. Chen has a positive attitude towards life and has been actively participating in charity work for the minority groups and the physically challenged. In May 2011, it was his eleventh time climbed up the mountain of Tai Shan.

About TED/TEDx

Ideas worth spreading

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience.

At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event.

The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. Started as a four-day conference in California 25 years ago, TED has grown to support those world-changing ideas with multiple initiatives.

The annual TED Conference invites the world’s leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes. Their talks are then made available, free, at TED.com.

TED speakers have included Bill Gates, Al Gore, Jane Goodall, Elizabeth Gilbert, Sir Richard Branson, Nandan Nilekani, Philippe Starck, Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala, Isabel Allende and former UK Prime Minister Gordon Brown.

The annual TED Conference takes place each spring in Long Beach, California, along with the TEDActive simulcast in Palm Springs; the annual TEDGlobal conference is held each summer in Edinburgh, Scotland.

TED's media initiatives include TED.com, where new TEDTalks are posted daily, the recently launched TED-Ed platform for students and educators.
